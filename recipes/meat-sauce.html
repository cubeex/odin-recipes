Old Italian Meat Sauce

This Italian meat sauce with mushrooms is robust and hearty. Serve over your favorite pasta with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Ingredients

  1. Two {2} pounds of lean ground beef
  2. One {1} pound of ground pork
  3. Two {2} tablespoons of olive oil
  4. Two {2} onions, chopped
  5. One {1} clove of garlic, crushed
  6. Three {3} cups of red wine, divided
  7. Two {2} pounds of fresh mushrooms, sliced
  8. Four {4} tablespoons of chopped fresh oregano
  9. One fourth {1/4} teaspoon of dried rosemary
  10. One fourth {1/4} teaspoon of chopped fresh thyme
  11. Three {3} (29 ounce) cans of tomato sauce
  12. One {1} (6 ounce) can of tomato paste

Steps

  1. This Italian meat sauce with mushrooms is robust and hearty. Serve over your favorite pasta with Parmesan cheese if desired.
  2. Warm olive oil in another large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic in hot oil until onions are tender, about 5 minutes. Pour in 1/2 cup wine; mix well.
  3. Add mushrooms, oregano, rosemary, and thyme to onion mixture. Pour in another 1/2 cup wine; cook and stir until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add browned meat, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to mushroom mixture. Reduce the heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  5. Pour in remaining 2 cups wine. Continue simmering for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally; serve.
